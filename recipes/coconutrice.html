Coconut Rice Recipe

Coconut rice is a tasty rice dish

It is a very popular delicacy with different claim of origin

Ingridents

  • Long grain rice - 500g
  • Coconut - 1 big size
  • Vegetable oil - half a cup
  • vegetables - carrot, green peas as desired
  • Salt - to taste
  • Seasoning cube - 4 cubes of knorr chicken cubes
  • Meat - as desired (I prefare chicken)
  • Water - 4 cups

Steps

  1. Break and slice the coconut as desired
  2. Boil the long grain rice for 10 minutes and drain the water then put aside
  3. put pot on the gas stove and allow to heat for 1 minute
  4. put Vegetable oil inside the pot
  5. Add the per boiled rice, coconut water and water. Cover to cook for 10 minutes
  6. Add the sliced coconut and cover to cook for 10minutes
  7. Boil the meat and fry or cook as desired
  8. Serve the Coconut rice with meat and some vegetables
