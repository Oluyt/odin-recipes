Fried Rice Recipe

Fried rice is a classy rice dish

It is a very popular delicacy with different cooking styles

Ingridents

  • Long grain rice - 500g
  • Sweet corn - 200g tin
  • Carrot - as desired
  • Green peas - as desired
  • Vegetable oil - half a cup
  • Salt - to taste
  • Seasoning cube - 4 cubes of knorr chicken cubes
  • Curry powder - 1 tea spoon
  • tyme seasoning - as desired
  • Meat - as desired (I prefare chicken)
  • Water - 4 cups

Steps

  1. Boil the long grain rice for 10 minutes and drain the water then put aside
  2. put pot on the gas stove and allow to heat for 1 minute
  3. put Vegetable oil inside the pot
  4. Add curry powder, type seasoning and the seasoning cubes
  5. Add the per boiled rice and water then cover to cook for 30 minutes
  6. Add sweet corn, carrot and green peas. cover for 5 minutes
  7. Boil the meat and fry or cook as desired
  8. Serve the fried rice with meat and some vegetables
