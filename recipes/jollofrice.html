Jollof Rice Recipe

Jollof rice is a tasty western african rice dish

It is a very popular delicacy of western african origin

Ingridents

  • Long grain rice - 500g
  • Pepper - as desired
  • Tomatoes - as desired
  • Onions - as desired
  • Tomato paste - 2 sachets or 1 medium size tin
  • Vegetable oil - half a cup
  • Salt - to taste
  • Seasoning cube - 4 cubes of knorr chicken cubes
  • Curry powder - 1 tea spoon
  • Meat - as desired (I prefare chicken)
  • Water - 4 cups

Steps

  1. Blend pepper, tomatoes and onions together and put aside
  2. Boil the long grain rice for 10 minutes and drain the water then put aside
  3. put pot on the gas stove and allow to heat for 1 minute
  4. put Vegetable oil inside the pot
  5. Add the blended pepper, tomatoes and onions
  6. Add tomato paste
  7. Add curry powder
  8. Add water and cover till the pot till boiling point
  9. Add the per boiled rice and cover to cook for 30 minutes
  10. Boil the meat and fry or cook as desired
  11. Serve the jollof rice with meat and some vegetables
